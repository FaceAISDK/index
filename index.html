隐私政策Privacy Policy

我们高度重视您的隐私。以下是关于 FaceAISDK 如何处理数据的核心说明：

  • 本地推断：FaceAISDK 是完全离线的 SDK。所有人脸检测、识别及活体检测过程均在用户设备本地完成。
  • 数据不上传：我们不收集、不存储、不上传任何图像数据、生物识别特征或个人信息到我们的服务器。
  • 数据控制：使用本 SDK 的集成商（开发者）完全拥有并控制其应用内产生的所有数据，我们无法访问这些数据。

We value your privacy. Here is how FaceAISDK handles data:

  • On-device Processing: FaceAISDK is an offline SDK. All face detection, recognition, and liveness checks are performed locally on the user's device.
  • No Data Upload: We do NOT collect, store, or upload any images, biometric templates, or personal information to our servers.
  • Data Control: Developers integrating our SDK have full ownership and control over the data generated within their apps. We have no access to this data.
